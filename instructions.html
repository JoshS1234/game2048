How to play

Step 1

There are some numbers on the board

Step 2

Press any direction (Up, down, left, right) to move all tiles.

Step 3

A new tile of 2 or 4 will appear on the board after every move

Step 4

When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!

Step 5

When the board is filled up, you lose.

Step 6

When a 2048 tile is created, you win!
